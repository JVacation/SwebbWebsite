About Swebb

SWEBB is a Design and Build company that is based in Brentwood and operates in the surrounding area. SWEBB offers an all in one package when it comes to turning your ideas into a reality and are with our customers every step of the way. SWEBB are open to all ideas and possiblities and we are certain that we will be able to offer the service and result that you want. Our workforce are all fully trained and follow the correct procedures when developing projects to ensure that we are able to provide you with what you expect from us. No job is too big or too small for us and we pride in making your experience as stress free and enjoyable as possible.

Andy Webb - Construction

A Carpenter and Joiner by trade, I have had my own Bespoke Joinery business for over 30 years, supervising teams of carpenters and I also have a vast experience of site management.

Previous projects, to name a few, include, the restoration of St George’s Chapel in Windsor Castle following the tragic fire, work at the Palace of Westminster, Brighton Pavilion, The National Gallery and 10 Downing Street. I have worked on many more ‘listed’ and important buildings all over the country often through English Heritage.

As a result of this experience I have gained a good knowledge of most trades and will use this extensive experience to site manage your project with the utmost professionalism.

My work on previous prestigious projects needed to be of a very high standard and it is our aim to bring this same high standard and apply it to any contract we are involved with
